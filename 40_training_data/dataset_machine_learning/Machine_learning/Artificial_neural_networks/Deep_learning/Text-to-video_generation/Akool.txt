Akool Inc. is an American company founded in 2022 and headquartered in Palo Alto, California, that specializes in generative artificial intelligence research and technologies. The company creates products and models for generating videos, images, and various multimedia content.

History

The company was founded in 2022 by Jiajun (Jeff) Lu, who previously worked at Google as a technical lead.

In 2025, it was ranked first on the Inc. 5000.

Services and technologies

Akool offers AI-powered tools for content creation, with emphasis on video generation and visual marketing applications.
