The World Artificial Intelligence Conference (WAIC) held in Shanghai, is one of China's largest conferences centered around artificial intelligence. The first WAIC was held in 2018.

The 8th WAIC was held in 2025 with the theme of "Global Solidarity in the AI Era". It attracted over 800 exhibitors showcasing 3,000+ exhibits, according to the organizers. Over 350,000 visits were reported.
